Keep track of student class report

Tracking student progress plays a key role in being an effective teacher. Having students see their growth and review how it relates to bigger goals increases student investment. Plus, tracking helps you, as the teacher, stay invested and continue to increase your own effectiveness by seeing trends in student data.

Keep track of student technical report

Create a spreadsheet to track each student's technical report. Include columns for the student's name, the topic of their report, the due date, and the status (in progress, submitted, graded, etc.). Update the spreadsheet regularly to keep track of each student's progress.

Keep track of student sports/extra-curriculum activities report

Create a tracking system to keep track of each student's sports/extra-curricular activities. You can use a spreadsheet or a software tool designed for this purpose.Set up a system to receive regular updates from students on their sports/extra-curricular activities. This can be done through a weekly check-in, an email update, or a regular report. Make sure that students know what is expected of them and how to submit their updates.
